Digital Shop – How to Delete Your Data

If you want to delete the data that Digital Shop stores about you, please follow the steps below inside the Digital Shop app.

Delete all your data in Digital Shop

  1. Open the Digital Shop app.
  2. Go to Product Groups and delete your product groups.
  3. Go to Company and delete your company data.
  4. Go to User Profile and delete your user profile.

Completing all of the steps above will delete your product, company, and profile information from Digital Shop.
