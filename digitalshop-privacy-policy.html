Digital Shop – Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 2025

Digital Shop (“we”, “our”, “the app”) is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use the Digital Shop mobile application.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Information you provide directly

  • Email address – used for login and account recovery.
  • Password – stored securely using industry-standard hashing.
  • Profile details – such as name, company information, or product groups that you create inside the app.

1.2 Social login data

If you choose to sign in using Google or Facebook:

  • We may receive your name, email address, and basic profile information.
  • No additional data is collected without your permission.

1.3 Automatically collected data

Digital Shop may collect basic technical information such as:

  • Device type
  • Operating system version
  • App usage for performance and troubleshooting

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used for the following purposes:

  • To allow you to log in and use the app
  • To store your product groups, company details, and profile information
  • To provide customer support when needed
  • To improve app reliability and performance

We never sell or rent your data to third parties.

3. How Your Data Is Stored

  • Your password is stored using secure hashing.
  • Your data is stored in a protected database on a private server.
  • We take reasonable technical steps to safeguard your information.

4. Sharing of Your Information

Digital Shop does not share your personal information with third parties, except when required to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect the rights or security of Digital Shop and its users

5. Your Data Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the data we store about you
  • Request correction of incorrect information
  • Request deletion of your data
